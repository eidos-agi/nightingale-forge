Nightingale reads the wreckage and writes the warning on the door.

Named for Florence Nightingale, who didn't just nurse soldiers — she studied WHY they were dying, invented data visualization to prove it, and changed medicine because the evidence was undeniable.

This forge teaches agents to study failures systematically: read session transcripts, extract incidents, catalog them with structured evidence, cross-reference against governance runbooks (StepProof), and surface unprotected failure modes before they repeat.

The output is not a report. It's a living incident corpus that every agent consults before risky operations. Every study run makes the corpus richer. Every gap report makes the runbooks tighter. The forge is the machine; the incidents are the compound interest.
